Understanding Our Pricing

At Miguel Lawn Services, we believe in offering clear, honest, and competitive pricing for every service we provide. Our goal is to make professional lawn care accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of quality. The prices listed above represent starting rates, which may vary depending on the size of your property, the condition of the lawn, and the specific requirements of each project. We always strive to give our customers the best value in the area, combining fair pricing with reliable, detailed work.

Every service we offer includes professional tools, careful preparation, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Lawn mowing prices depend on yard size, mowing frequency, and whether edging or trimming is needed. Mulch replacement varies based on the type of mulch selected, the depth required, and the total square footage of the garden beds. Planting flowers or bushes may involve soil preparation, plant selection, and additional materials, which can influence the final cost. We always provide personalized estimates so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

Seasonal services also affect pricing. For example, spring planting requires fresh soil, fertilizers, and careful placement to ensure strong root development. Summer services often involve weekly or biweekly mowing, weed control, and lawn fertilization to maintain healthy growth. Autumn cleanup includes leaf removal, bush trimming, and seed planting to prepare your yard for winter. Winter services may include snow removal, gutter cleaning, and debris clearing to keep your property safe during cold weather. Each season has unique needs, and our pricing reflects the time, materials, and labor required to deliver excellent results.

We understand that every property is different. Some lawns require more frequent maintenance, while others may need special treatments such as weed control, fertilization, or soil improvement. If you are unsure which service is right for your yard, we are happy to visit your property, evaluate its condition, and recommend the best options based on your goals and budget. Our mission is to help you maintain a clean, healthy, and beautiful outdoor space all year long.

Transparency is important to us. We never add hidden fees or unexpected charges. Before starting any job, we explain the process, the materials involved, and the estimated time required. This ensures you feel confident and informed every step of the way. Our customers appreciate our honesty, reliability, and dedication to delivering high-quality lawn care at affordable prices.

Thank you for considering Miguel Lawn Services. We take pride in serving the Lanham, Maryland community and nearby areas with dependable lawn care and landscaping services. Whether you need a one-time cleanup or ongoing maintenance, we are here to help. Contact us anytime for a free estimate, and let us show you why we offer the best prices and service in the area.
